Technology

With the magic of 3D printing, any idea can become reality.

  • Step 1:

    An Idea

    A 3D modeler knows he has the next big thing, and gets to work creating a 3D model of his idea on a computer.

  • Step 2:

    Local to Public

    Said design is then uploaded to Shapeways.com, where people like you, as well as other designers, can see them as well as purchase a physical object of that model.

  • Step 3:

    Digital to Physical

    Once an order is placed, the 3D model is sent to Shapeways factories where is it physically produced with industrial-sized, selective-lazer-sintering 3D printers. Depending on the material ordered, the object is tumbled, polished, or dyed.

  • Step 4:

    Delivery

    After processing, the physical model is delivered straight to your door, hand packaged and ready to go.

About Us

Meet me and the brand.

Chris Fischer

Designer

I am a seventeen year-old self-taught 3D modeler with a passion for design. I started this shop in 2012 when looking for a necklace for my mom with three hearts. Although I was unsuccessful in my search for the piece, I found something I didn't know I had, wanted, or enjoyed: 3D modeling and the best part, printing. Thanks to Shapeways, I am able to create jewelry designs without knowledge of metal working or casting. And with all of that striped away, what is left is the just the artist and his idea. Just pure creativity.

Fischfluous
